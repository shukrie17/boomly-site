Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Introduction

Boomly respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

Information We Collect

  • Email address when creating an account.
  • Authentication information through Firebase Authentication.
  • Location information used to find nearby coffee shops.
  • Saved favourite cafés stored on your device.
  • Basic app usage information for performance and security.

How We Use Information

  • Provide coffee shop recommendations.
  • Enable account login and security.
  • Save favourite cafés.
  • Improve app performance.
  • Provide customer support.

Location Data

Boomly uses your device location only to show nearby coffee shops and calculate distances. Location information is not sold to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Boomly uses Google Places API, Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore and Google Sign-In.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, disclosure, or alteration.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting Boomly Support.
